Traditional Arts Exhibition in Jakarta

Date: March 15, 2025

A traditional arts exhibition will be held at the National Gallery of Jakarta. This exhibition will showcase various artworks from different regions of Indonesia, including batik, wayang (puppet), and traditional musical instruments.

Cultural Arts Festival in Bali

Date: April 20, 2025

The annual cultural arts festival in Bali will feature traditional dance performances, gamelan music, and craft exhibitions. This event aims to preserve and promote Balinese culture to the younger generation.

Batik Workshop for Beginners

Date: May 5, 2025

A batik workshop will be held in Yogyakarta, where participants can learn the basic techniques of batik-making from local artisans. This is a great opportunity to gain a deeper understanding of batik art, which is a cultural heritage of Indonesia.
