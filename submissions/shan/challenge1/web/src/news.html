Phantom Raises $150 Million in Series C Funding Round, Valued at $3 Billion

By FinanceFeeds • 19 hours ago • 2 mins read

Digital asset wallet provider Phantom has successfully raised $150 million in a Series C funding round. The round was led by prominent investors Sequoia Capital and Paradigm, with participation from Andreessen Horowitz and Variant. This investment round values Phantom at an impressive $3 billion, marking a 150% increase from its previous valuation of $1.2 billion in early 2022.

Phantom is a leading wallet solution for the Solana (SOL) ecosystem, renowned for its user-friendly interface and seamless integration with decentralized applications (dApps). The funding aims to accelerate product development, enhance user experience, and expand its reach beyond Solana into multi-chain functionality.

With the growing adoption of digital assets, Phantom's rapid rise underscores the increasing demand for secure and efficient crypto wallets. The company's CEO expressed optimism about the future, stating that these funds will enable Phantom to scale its operations and bring innovative solutions to the Web3 ecosystem.
