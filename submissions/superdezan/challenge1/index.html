About Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a type of digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security. It is decentralized and operates on blockchain technology, which makes it secure and resistant to manipulation. The most popular cryptocurrency is Bitcoin, but many other altcoins like Ethereum, Ripple, and Litecoin exist as well.
